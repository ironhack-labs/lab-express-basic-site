Hurakami was born in Kyoto on the 12th of January 1949.

From an early age on Murakami was heavily influenced by Western culture, reading a wide range of European and American literature, such as Franz Kafka, Charles Dickens and Fyodor Dostokyevsky. He studied Drama at Waseda University and before finishing his degree, he opened a bar with his wife Yoko. The bar was openf from 1974 to 1981. According to different sources, the couple decided not to have children. Murakami discovered his passion for running when he was in his 30s, in 1996 he even completed his first ultramarathon, a 100km race around Lake Saroma in Hokkaido, Japan.

Murakami started writing when he was 29 years old, his inspiration for his first novel "Hear the Wind sing" was a baseball game. As the novel was quite successful, he kept on going and published a sequel Pinball. In 1987 he had a major breakthrough with his publication "Norwegian Wood".

Murakami has won several prizes such as the Franz Kafka Prize, Tanizaki Prize, World Fantasy Award and so on.
