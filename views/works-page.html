Jon Krakauer

Jon Krakauer (Brookline, Massachusetts; April 12, 1954) is an American journalist, writer, and mountaineer, renowned for his books on mountaineering. In 2003 he ventured into the field of investigative journalism. Krakauer was born in Brookline, Massachusetts but grew up in Corvallis, Oregon, from the age of two. He was the third of five brothers. He competed in tennis at Corvallis High School and graduated in 1972. He studied at Hampshire College in Massachusetts, earning his degree in environmental studies in 1976. In 1977, he met rock climber Linda Mariam Moore, whom he would marry three years later.
