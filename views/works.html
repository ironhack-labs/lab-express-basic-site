Work

  • 1938 - Lynn Margulis was born in Chicago, to a Jewish, Zionist family.
  • 1966 - Margulis wrote a theoretical paper titled "On the Origin of Mitosing Cells", predecesor of the modern endosymbiotic theory.
  • 1983 - Margulis was elected a member of the US National Academy of Sciences
  • 2008 - President Bill Clinton presented her the National Medal of Science in 1999. The Linnean Society of London awarded her the Darwin-Wallace Medal
  • 2008 - Margulis dies on November 22
