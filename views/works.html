Why is Rowlet the cutest pokémon?

As you might have already noticed, Rowlet is a cinnamon roll.
It's round, like a ball, tiny and cuddly. It can fit right in your backpack and you can carry it anywhere and everywhere.
But Rowlet isn't only cute. It's also AWESOME!
It evolves into one of the coolest pokémon, Decidueye, a grass/ghost pokémon.
But it doesn't need evolution to be awesome! (ask Ash). Even if it does't like it, it'll help you kick Groudon's ass!
