Who's Carlton Banks?

Carlton is a world-renowned sexual healing guru, whose unique soul-blooming dance technique earned him a Canonisation from the Vatican in 2014.

Early years

Baby Carlton was born on April the 1st 1988 to an upper class family in Bel Air, Los Angeles, USA. He was a beloved and cherished child and enjoyed a happy childhood, which would set the first steps towards his brilliant career.

Late teens: Great expectations

Despite his limited stature, young Carlton would develop a massive talent for sports, mainly basketball. This joyful era, however, would not last. His career would end abruptly on 2005, when, on an international match against the Republic of the Congo, he would break both kneecaps on an unfortunate clash with an audience member.

From the pitch to the spotlight

That accident would mark the end of Carlton's career as an elite athlete, but would only be the start of his notoriety. Having cemented his position as one of the US' greatest personalities, Carlton would be a regular at all of Hollywood's parties, sharing the bling with the brightests stars of the moment.

Bad boy era

Sharing the bling, unfortunately, led to Carlton rubbing shoulders amongst some not-so-good people, and taking part in shady deals, not for wealth, but for the thrill of it. This downward spiral would end with Carlton serving 10 years in jail for manslaughter and indecent exposure.

A new man emerges from jail

The quietude of jail provided Carlton with the perfect athmosphere to reconsider his life choices. After getting in contact with a saucy latino philosopher inmate, Carlton started exploring the depths of his soul, and emerged as a renovated man, leading him to develop his trademark sexual healing dance methodology.

Canonisation

His works as a sexual healing guru lead to the miraculous healing and enhornyment of millions of people, leading the Catholic Church to officialy canonise him on 2014.
