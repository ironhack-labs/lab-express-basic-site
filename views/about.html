Frank

Frank was born in Cuba, and resides in Miami, FL. After 8 years of studying and taking art courses, his interest in the art of tattooing and alternative mediums has turned him into a multidisciplinary artist. Frank has built a professional career and portfolio with his tattoos. His style is unconventional and unique in Miami, as he mixes smooth shading with lines that resemble brush strokes and sketches.
