About Gollum

Origins

  • Gollum's original name was Smeagol
  • He was born as a Hobbit in year 2440 T.A.
  • While fishing with his cousin for his birthday in year 2463 T.A., they found the One and Unique Ring of Power. They will fight over it and Smeagol will kill his cousin Deagol... That is the start of Gollum's history
  • Gollum will get obsessed with the Ring and will spend centuries in a cave, the Ring giving him an infinite live but full of pain

In The Hobbit

  • In year 2941 T.A., as Sauron, the Ring's master, looks for his Ring, it flees form Gollum's lair so Bilbo can find it.
  • Gollum then intends to kill Bilbo in order to get the Ring back.
  • But Bilbo manages to escape by wearing the Ring that makes him invisible to Gollum's eyes.

In The Lord of the Ring

  • Gollum will end up helping Frodo, Bilbo's son, in his quest to destroying the Ring, by showing him the way to Mordor (the only place where the Ring can be destroyed, in Sauron's land where he built it)
  • During the whole trip, Gollum is struggling with his former personnality of Smeagol and they have an internal fight to whether kill Frodo to get the Ring back or not.
  • In the end, Gollum's will wins over Smeagol's and they will try to kill Frodo before he destroys the Ring, but they will die in the attempt detroy the Ring at the same time...
