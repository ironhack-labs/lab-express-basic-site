Paul David Hewson known by his stage name Bono is an Irish singer-songwriter and musician. He is best known as the lead vocalist and primary lyricist of rock band U2.

Bono was born and raised in Dublin, Ireland. He attended Mount Temple Comprehensive School where he met his future wife, Alison Stewart, as well as schoolmates with whom he founded U2 in 1976.

Bono soon established himself as a passionate frontman for the band through his expressive vocal style and grandiose gestures and songwriting.

As a member of U2, Bono has received 22 Grammy Awards and has been inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
