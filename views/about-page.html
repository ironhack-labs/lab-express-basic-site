About dat boi

Dat Boi (coll: "that boy") is a nickname given to a 3D character model of a green frog riding a unicycle, typically accompanied by catchphrases like "here comes dat boi!" and "o **** waddup!" that went viral in early 2016

Some claim that Dat Boi's origins can be found in an AP PHYSICS book as a problem's illustration, but it is uncertain if the meme came from the illustration or viceversa.

As is evidenced in a viral Reddit copypasta, some people believe Dat Boi to be the paragon of a "self made meme", claiming that: The first person to ever upvote dat boi upvoted a meme literally pulled from the ether by sheer human creativity and willpower. Dat boi is evidence that humans can stare into the meaningless void of eternity and force their own meaning onto to it. I will always upvote dat boi, o **** waddup!"
