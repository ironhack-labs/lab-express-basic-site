ABOUT US

BOULEVARD DES AIRS was born in 2004 in Tarbes, Hautes-Pyrenees. Boulevard des Airs is an eclectic French band responsible for a melting pot of rock, reggae, ska, Latin, and Balkan sounds. It is a nine-piece band : guitar, bass, drums, trumpet, trombone, saxophone, keyboards, accordion. They carry on cheerful songs with conscious French, Spanish or English lyrics sung in French.

Their first record (Paris-Buenos Aires), released in 2011, is now gold record (+ 50 000 copies sold). The videoclip of their first single “Cielo Ciego” has reached 1 000000 views on Youtube and has been broadcast by following channels : Virgin Radio, France 2, NRJ, M6, W9, TV5 Monde, MTV Pulse. They played more than 100 gigs in 2012, touring in France, Spain, Belgium, Switzerland, Canada playing alonside international artists such as Damian Marley, Shaka Ponk, The Offspring, Aerosmith, Ska P.

This tour has been nominated for Les Victoires de la Musique in 2013. Their second record was released in 2013, followed by 18 months of touring which started in Paris Bercy where they performed as the opening act for the French band Tryo. Inspired by Manu Chao/Mano Negra, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Tryo, Marley, Ska P, their music is best described as alternative rock.
