About The Best Band Ever

History

Jamiroquai is a British funk and jazz band from London, formed in 1991. They debuted as an acid-jazz band and have since explored other musical directions such as pop, rock, disco and electronic.

Members

Fronted by singer-songwriter Jay Kay,the band's best-known line-up was additionally composed of co-songwriter Toby Smith (keyboard), Stuart Zender (bass), Derrick McKenzie (drums) and Wallis Buchanan (vibraphone/didgeridoo). The group has changed its line-up several times; with McKenzie and Sola Akingbola (percussion), who both joined in 1994, still in the official line-up.

Legendary Song

Love Foolosophy is one of the most legendary Jamiroquai's songs. This one was so popular that they launched a live version recorded on the famous Abbey Road Studios at London City. Great recommendation for the ultimate fans. Simply unforgettable version.
