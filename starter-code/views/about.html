The about page

Elon Musk originates from south Africa where he was born on june 6th 1971 in Pretoria.
But Elon isn't the man of a single country so he changed nationality to become canadian in 1989 and then american since 2002.

He graduated in sciences and arts (at the same time according to Wikipedia, what a man!)

He's become a famous entrepreneur with his high-tech companies and his ambitious projects which are either already launched (Tesla cars, solar tiles and wall batteries) or still to come (travel to Mars, very high speed train Hyperloop...)
