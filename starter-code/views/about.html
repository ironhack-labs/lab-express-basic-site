Hello on our about page!

BUYmeCAR is a digital automotive marketplace dedicated to being the most transparent brand in the industry. BUYmeCAR shows consumers what others paid for the car they want, so they can recognize a fair price. Users receive upfront pricing information when they connect with BUYmeCAR Certified Dealers, allowing them to enjoy a more confident buying experience. BUYmeCAR operates its own site and powers car-buying programs for over 500 companies, including some of the most trusted brands in the world such as USAA, AARP and American Express. BUYmeCAR has a network of over 14,000 Certified Dealers and currently, over one third of all new car buyers engage with the BUYmeCAR network during their purchasing process.

At buymecar we understand that selling a car can be a difficult, confusing and time-consuming process. Selling your car is something that you don’t do every day, so it’s difficult to know the best way to go about it. If you do connect with potential buyers through your ad, how can you be sure which ones are seriously interested and not simply going to waste your time trying to get an unreasonable bargain and then move on to the next potential victim? There’s no way of telling until your time and energy have already been spent. And, anyhow, do you really want strangers coming over to your home? Even if it does get as far as a 'buyer' actually getting behind the wheel of your vehicle for a test drive, who can guarantee that they won't damage your car in the process, leaving you with a costly repair bill or an increase in your insurance premium? And if you do make the sale, your worries still aren't over as the buyer’s check may bounce. Then you’re stuck without a car - or your payment! How about going to a dealership to sell your car? Unless your bargaining skills are top notch, you're in for quite a hassle trying to get what your car is worth. Dealers are experts who have years of experience at beating you down on price: that's how they make a profit! When you are “dealing with a dealer” there’s no guarantee of a fair price. You can’t be sure you’re getting a legitimate valuation, especially if your car is a different brand than those offered by the particular dealership.
