women + salad + aloneness = unmitigated ecstasy

The divine magic resulting from the union of these three conditions continues to puzzle scientists and leading religious thinkers today.

women

Women are adult female humans. They have two x chromosomes.

salad

A salad is a human food dish made usually of lettuce or other leafy greens, raw vegetables, and a dressing often made of oil and vinegar.

aloneness

Aloneness is the human state of not being in the immediate physical vicinity of other humans.
