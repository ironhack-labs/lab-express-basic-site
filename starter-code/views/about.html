About Alan Rickman


Alan Sidney Patrick Rickman (21 February 1946 â€“ 14 January 2016) was an English actor and director. Rickman trained at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London and became a member of the Royal Shakespeare Company, performing in modern and classical theatre productions. Rickman's first film role was as the German terrorist leader Hans Gruber in Die Hard (1988).

Other film roles included:

  • the Sheriff of Nottingham in Robin Hood: Prince of Thieves (1991)
  • Jamie in Truly, Madly, Deeply (1990)
  • P.L. O'Hara in An Awfully Big Adventure (1995)
  • Colonel Brandon in Sense and Sensibility (1995)
  • Harry in Love Actually (2003)

Rickman gained further notice for his film performances as Severus Snape in the Harry Potter series.
