About Green Tea

Green Tea is a true tea type made using the leaves of the Camellia sinensis tea plant. It is called a true tea because it is actually made from the leaves of the plant, versus other herbal tea types that are made from the leaves, stems, roots and flowers of plants.

Health Benefits of Drinking Green Tea

Green tea is widely known for its beneficial properties. It contains antioxidants called polyphenols and catechins that boast a wide variety of health effects, including:

  • Improved blood circulation
  • Help lower high blood pressure
  • Help regulate blood sugar levels
  • Helps prevent growth and spread of some tumors/cancerous activity.
  • Helps slow memory regression and provides some protection from neurological diseases.
