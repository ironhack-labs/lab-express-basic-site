Introducing: My wife Petra - the artist

About us Petra's photo gallery

Petra has been making sculptures since the eighties, initially towerlike structures finished with adobe and water color, inspired by work by the Austrian architect Hundertwasser, but also by African art and architecture.

Lately she started making small highly decorative ceramic bowls that would look good in any interior.

Now that I'm doing bootcamp all day she has all the time in the world to work long hours, creating more and more beautiful sculptures. like I do.

Last summer she was artist in residence at the Irish residence of the Polranny Pirates. A description of what she did there will be published soon.
